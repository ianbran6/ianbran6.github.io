About

Welcome to Ian Branam's professional portfolio.

Ian is a graduate student in Health & Medical Journalism at the University of Georgia and a freelance writer for health and science publications. He will complete his M.A. degree in May 2014. He graduated Magna Cum Laude from the University of Georgia in 2012 with Bachelor's degrees in History and Psychology.

Ian aims to work in the field of health communication to educate people on health risks and promote healthy behaviors. He has written for publications ranging from local news sites like Athens Patch to national publications like Scientific American.
